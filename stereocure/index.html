Savage Casual, by Kuh-Lida

Spheres, by Christopher Knollmeyer

REDYELLOWVIOLET, by RYV

Stereocure is a record label based in Los Angeles, California, and a partner of Belgian Man Records.

Christopher Knollmeyer's most recent project, Squares & Spheres, was released as two mini-albums: Squares, on Belgian Man, and Spheres, on Stereocure.

Kuh-Lida's Savage Casual, and RYV's REDYELLOWVIOLET were co-released by Stereocure and Belgian Man.
