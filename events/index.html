Cloud Party

A. Campbell Payne performed a piece titled Cloud Party as part of Figment Boston on Saturday 7/28/12 on the Greenway.

It's a piece for solo performer and audience using the sky + shapes of clouds as a graphic score for performance. The shapes of the clouds and individual interpretations of how to sonify those shapes results in radically different performances depending on the weather, mood of the performers, geographical location, overall social vibe, etc.

Imma let you Finnish

Imma let you Finnish was a concert held at Arjuntina on July 7, 2012. RTFM played an acoustic set, followed by Liz Wood, Scrub MC, DJ Kristöffer, Charlie Abbott, Christopher Knollmeyer, Kimbo Slice, and Luc Lacerte. Red Lips Big Hips closed the evening with a fantastic freestyle session, featuring Chris on the ones and the twos.

Official Belgian Man Photographer Michael Ciociola was on site, and his photos are available at Arjuntina.
