Amen, Hallelujah, Amen!

A. Campbell Payne is presenting a sound installation, titled Amen, Hallelujah, Amen!, at the 2012 Soundcrawl Festival in Nashville, TN

The drum break is one of the foundations of hip hop. Amen Hallelujah Amen attempts to reveal the hidden secrets of the break through rapid acceleration, diffusion, and granulation of the source material. The infamous "Amen" break acts as the eponym of this piece.

Cloud Party

A. Campbell Payne performed a piece titled Cloud Party as part of Figment Boston on Saturday 7/28/12 on the Greenway.

It's a piece for solo performer and audience using the sky + shapes of clouds as a graphic score for performance. The shapes of the clouds and individual interpretations of how to sonify those shapes results in radically different performances depending on the weather, mood of the performers, geographical location, overall social vibe, etc.
