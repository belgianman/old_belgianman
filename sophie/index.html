Sophie’s Basement
Good Night Sophie’s Basement, and Forever Farewell
The Final Battle?

Sophie’s Basement was a venue in Sudbury, MA.

It was run by Sophie Griswald.

Good Night Sophie’s Basement, and Forever Farewell was a concert held New Year’s Eve 2011. The lineup included Limelight, A. Campbell Payne, RTFM, the Big Trucks featuring Polar Bear, BR1GHT PR1MATE, Christopher Knollmeyer, Gavin Atkinson, DJ Kristöffer, Polytech, and i am (noun),

Sophie’s Basement Show Mk IV: The Final Battle? took place on May 28, 2011. It featured the poetry of Monastic Climax, and musical performances by The Taxidermists, DJ Kristöffer, uhpollo, Doomcloud, and i am (noun)
