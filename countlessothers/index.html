Countless Others

Releases

Countless Others is the musickal electronickal sing song project of Sam Fisher, and formerly Charlie Abbott.

Their first EP, Fantastickal Worldz, was released in July, 2011. It was reviewed on July 23rd 2012 by Nick Mirin for Pop Veggies.

Their newest single with Belgian Man, How Does It Feel? was released in March, 2012.
