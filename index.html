Belgian Man Records

Belgian Man Records is a collective of artists that began in lovely, historic Concord, MA, but is currently based in Chicago, IL.

We record music, film videos, make art, and play shows.

All of our music is licensed under a Creative Commons by-nc license, which means you’re free to share, remix, and re-use it non-commercially, provided you give attribution to the artist. If you’d like to use Belgian Man music commercially, email Andrew.

If you’re trying to write about Belgian Man, you should probably email the fabulous Catherine Pears.

hwut

Original Belgian Man RYV came through from Japan with this incredible sonic collage, replete with beautiful textures, blips, and sounds. He’s been working round the clock to develop this sonic explosion. It’s finally ready to hit your eardrums.

RYV is Charlie Abbott, repping Oberlin, OH from Tokyo, Japan. Fans of Lapalux will appreciate his ability to create ethereal, immersive aural landscapes, gained from years of worship at the altar of sound.

High Adventure

Holy shit peep this STELLAR new tape by Belgian Man favorite, Kuh-Lida

This new collection of fuzzed-out breaks and radio drama collages from the back catalog of Stereocure co-founder Kuh-Lida (aka Myles Emmons) sometimes reads more like a sound walk through his own thrift store than a beat tape. Veiled behind layers of dust and tape hiss, the veteran producer presents us with a sound world that he has created out of found objects and hazy imagination, shattered bits of magnetic tape and broadcasted nostalgia. Directing our senses through a maze of swerves and spin-outs at every turn, the music somehow fits together like a jigsaw puzzle, or a vinyl record chopped into a hundred pieces and reassembled with paint and peanut butter. Through thickets of glitch and throbbing blotches of rhythm, this A/B tape release is an auditory adventure from start to finish.
