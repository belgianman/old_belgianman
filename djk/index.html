Chris Rutledge has been making music since the day he was born. He's based in Providence, Rhode Island. He has released music as DJ Kristöffer and Tokyo Megaplex.

For his albums, Blunt Roller Coaster, Bassment Party, and Enter The Megaplex, Chris made individual art for each track of the album, so that as it plays in iTunes you can watch the cover change.
